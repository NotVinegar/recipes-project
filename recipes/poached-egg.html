Poached Egg

Poached eggs are a wonderful addition to ramen or toast, and this method makes them very easy to cook.

Ingredients:

  • Egg
  • Cling wrap
  • Spray oil

Steps:

  1. Place the clig wrap over a bowl or cup opening.
  2. Spray some oil on top of the cling wrap.
  3. Crack the egg into the cling wrap and twist the rest around it so it forms a ball.
  4. Place into boiling water for 4 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
