Kimchi Fried Rice

Delicious fried rice made with kimchi and an egg.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup kimchi, cut into small pieces
  • 150g/5.3oz bacon, cut into small pieces
  • 1 pack enoki mushrooms(200g/7oz) with root removed
  • 3 cups of leftover steamed white rice
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup kimchi juice from container of kimchi
  • 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1-2 tbsp roasted sesame seeds
  • 1/2 stalk green onion, thinly sliced
  • roasted seasoned seaweed, shredded

Steps:

  1. Preheat a pan or wok to medium high and spread around the cooking oil.
  2. Add the garlic and stir around quickly for about 10 seconds, then add the bacon and do the same until about half of it is cooked.
  3. Add the kimchi and stir it around until most of it is cooked.
  4. If you've decided to use mushrooms, add now and mix for a few seconds, then reduce the heat to medium-medium low.
  5. Add the rice and kimchi juice, then mix together thoroughly.
  6. Add the sesame oil and mix it thoroughly, then remove from the heat.
  7. once plated sprinkle with sesame seeds, green onion, and seaweed strips, then place the egg on top, cooked however you'd like.
